About the Department

The Department of Computer Science at Government Arts and Science College, Sendamangalam was established in 2023 with a vision to produce skilled IT professionals who can contribute to the technological advancement of our nation.

Our department offers a comprehensive undergraduate program with modern curriculum covering:

  • Programming Languages (C, C++, Java, Python)
  • Web Development & Database Management
  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Networking & Cyber Security
